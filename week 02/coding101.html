Recipe

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Ranch/ketchup
  1. Heat vegetable oil to 300 degrees F in heavy pot.
  2. Peel and cut potatoes into desired shape.
  3. In batches fry the cut potatoes, until golden brown.
  4. Remove them from heat and drain on fresh paper towels.
  5. Sprinkle the fries with salt and pepper, then dip into ranch or ketchup!
